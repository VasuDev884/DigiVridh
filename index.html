About DigiVridh

A human-centered solution for digital inclusion

DigiVridh is not just an app. It combines physical learning centers, trained peer facilitators, local communities, and a multilingual mobile experience to make digital learning easier for senior citizens.

🏠

Community Anchored

Learning happens in familiar spaces like RWAs, parks, community halls, and temples.

🤝

Trust-Driven

Peer facilitators called DigiDosts help seniors learn from people they already trust.

🌐

Multilingual

The platform supports local languages, voice-based guidance, and simple step-by-step learning.

The Problem

Why senior citizens are left behind

Many senior citizens want to use digital services, but fear, fraud, language barriers, and complex design make adoption difficult.

Technology AnxietyFear of pressing the wrong button or losing money online.
Language BarriersMany apps are not friendly for seniors who prefer regional languages.
Fraud RiskUPI scams, fake calls, phishing messages, and unsafe links create fear.
Lack of GuidanceSeniors often depend on family or strangers for digital help.
The Solution

An integrated model with physical + digital support

DigiVridh combines local centers, peer facilitators, retirement groups, travel clubs, and a mobile app into one practical ecosystem.

01

Digital Choupal Centres

Local neighborhood hubs with devices, internet, printed guides, and a safe practice environment.

02

DigiDost Network

Trusted peer facilitators from the same community make learning feel comfortable and personal.

03

Retirement & Yatra Groups

Existing social groups become natural learning communities for digital confidence.

04

Mobile App + CyberSafe

A simple multilingual app with practical modules, family support, and fraud-awareness training.
